ThreeTopo Privacy Policy

ThreeTopo is an iOS app that runs locally on your device. We do not collect personal information, we do not ask for account details, and we do not send your app data to external servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or account information.

How The App Works

ThreeTopo works entirely on your device.

App data stays local and is not sent to our servers or to third parties.

Analytics And Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies in the app.

Third-Party Services

ThreeTopo does not require account creation and does not rely on third-party services to process your personal data.
